Current Workflow
Patient calls Shifa Clinic Phone: 770-300-0067 requests an appointment.
They are asked to email their request to shifaappointmentduluth@icnarelief.org
Volunteer checks email request and logs them in excel manually
Provider confirms availability 1-2 weeks in advance
1-2 days before scheduled appointment, a volunteer calls the patient for availability and confirms their appointment.
Once confirmed, patient’s info is placed in Athena EMR for that specific provider.

AI-Powered "Request-Only" Portals 
Asynchronous Booking: Patients use a conversational AI (like Voiceoc or Interactly.ai) to submit their symptoms and preferred times. The AI does not book a slot; instead, it creates a "request" or "case" in your dashboard for staff to approve once provider availability is confirmed.
Clinical Triage: Systems like Fabric Health or Clearstep can perform AI triage during the request phase. 


AI Medical Receptionists (Phone-to-Waitlist) 
24/7 Intake: AI agents (e.g., Assort Health or Retell AI) can answer calls, gather patient demographics, reason for appointment, and manually "push" that data into your “database.”
